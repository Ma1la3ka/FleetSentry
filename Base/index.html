Core Features

Everything a fleet manager needs

📍

Live Route Tracking

Drivers select start and end points on a map. Admins approve trips in one tap. Both parties get real-time status notifications at every stage.

⛽

Fuel Cost Intelligence

Set your current fuel price in the backend. The system auto-calculates consumption based on distance, detours, and idle time — instantly flagging overspend.

🚨

Theft & Abuse Alerts

Unauthorized stops, off-route deviations, excessive idling, and after-hours vehicle use trigger instant alerts to the admin dashboard.

📊

Admin Dashboard

One view for your entire fleet — active drivers, vehicle types, trip history, cost summaries, and compliance scores across all workers.

🕐

Time & Distance Audit

Every trip logs actual time taken vs. expected, kilometres driven, and route efficiency — giving you undeniable data for performance reviews.

✅

Trip Approval Workflow

Workers request trips, admins approve or reject. No driver moves without authorization. Full audit trail for every journey.

How It Works

Simple. Powerful. Accountable.

01

Admin registers company

Sign up with your company name, fleet size, and fuel pricing. Your admin dashboard is instantly ready.

→
02

Workers join the fleet

Drivers register, select their company, and specify their vehicle type. They appear immediately on your admin roster.

→
03

Trip requested & approved

Driver picks start and end location on the map. Admin reviews and approves. Driver is notified and begins journey.

→
04

Live monitoring & auto-report

Admin tracks the trip in real time. On arrival, the system auto-notifies the admin with full fuel, time, and route data.
